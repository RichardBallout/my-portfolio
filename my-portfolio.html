What is SEO?

SEO stands for search engine optimisation. There are many different factors which influence where a page ranks on search engines when you make a search for something. SEO is the practice of trying to get a page to rank highly in a search engine for certain keywords and phrases.

What influences search engine rankings?

Getting a page to rank highly in a search engine is not as simple as just writing the content and, wabam, high ranking.

Factors which influence search engine rankings include:

  • The domain authority of the site. This basically means how reputable the site is.
  • Quality backlinks from other sites. Google will reward you if your page has been linked to from other reputable sites.
  • The inclusion of certain keywords and phrases which are relevant to the topic of the page.
  • How active the site is in terms of publishing content.
  • The alt tags on images in the content.
  • The title and meta decription on the page.
  • The code behind the site.
