What is SEO?

SEO stands for search engine optimisation. SEO is the process of optimising a page/site so that it ranks highly in search engines for certain keywords and phrases.

There are many different factors which influence where a page ranks on search engines for certain keywords and phrases. Interestingly, the coding behind a site has a significant impact on search engine rankings, and much more than many people realise.
